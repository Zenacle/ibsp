About the Course

Course Overview

Buildings are more complex than any single discipline can cover. This programme connects the dots.

A modern building involves dozens of systems — HVAC, electrical, plumbing, fire protection, building automation, sustainability infrastructure — each designed by different specialists, each with its own logic. No single degree covers all of them.

What IBSP gives you is the integrated view. Across 12 weeks and 36 live sessions, you'll build a working understanding of how each major building system functions — and more importantly, how they influence each other in a real project environment.

If you're early in your career and looking to broaden your technical range, this programme gives you a foundation that makes you more effective in whatever role you're looking for — on site, in consulting, in sustainability, or beyond.

Programme Structure

Course Curriculum

7 comprehensive modules designed to take you from fundamentals to integration.

01
Built Environment & Construction Ecosystem
  • Introduction to the Built Environment
  • Types of buildings and lifecycle of a building project
  • Stakeholders: Architect, Structural, MEP, PMC, Contractors, OEMs
  • Project workflow: drawings, BOQ, contracts, tendering
  • Coordination between disciplines and integrated design approach
02
Plumbing & Water Management Systems
  • Water supply sources and demand basics
  • Storage systems and pump basics
  • Pipe materials and fittings
  • Drainage systems and routing basics
  • STP, ETP and water reuse concepts
  • Water efficiency and metering basics
03
HVAC Systems & Thermal Comfort
  • Thermal comfort fundamentals
  • Types of AC systems: Split, VRF, Chiller
  • AHU, FCU basics
  • Air distribution and ventilation
  • HVAC controls and efficiency basics
04
Fire Protection & Life Safety Systems
  • Fire fundamentals and risks
  • Fire extinguishers, hydrants, sprinklers
  • Fire detection systems
  • Emergency response basics
05
Electrical, ELV & Smart Building Systems
  • Electrical distribution basics
  • Lighting systems and efficiency
  • ELV systems: CCTV, Access control
  • Building Management Systems
  • Introduction to IoT and smart buildings
06
Green Buildings & Sustainable Design
  • Green building fundamentals
  • LEED and IGBC overview
  • Energy efficiency basics
  • Water conservation basics
  • Indoor environmental quality
07
Integrated Building Systems Approach
  • System integration concepts
  • Digital buildings overview
  • Facility management basics
  • Net Zero and smart infrastructure trends
